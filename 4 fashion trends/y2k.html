Y2K fashion is a style from the late 90’s to early 2000’s. The style is the fusion of pop culture and technology advancements, the era where the internet became popular. By utilizing bright colors and futuristic-esque clothing, anyone can dress themselves up in the Y2K aesthetics. Additionally, this style pushes for playfulness and youthfulness, something that makes you feel nostalgic of a more innocent time. The use of shiny materials, pleated skirts, baguette bags, low-waisted jeans, velour tracksuits, and more are some of the pieces that make up this Y2K aesthetic.


Pleated Mini Skirt

Mini skirts worn with knee socks and cropped cardigans are great combinations to bring out the Y2K feels.

Colorful Nails

Colorful nail art surely adds height to the Y2K aesthetics!

Baguette bags are flexible in completing the Y2K looks, both casually and formally. They are small enough to fit under your arm, yet can still carry many things. Varied in different styles and colors, baguette bags can be mixed and matched in many ways.
