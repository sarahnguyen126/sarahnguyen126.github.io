Double Cut-Out

Double cut-out layers can be pretty interesting too!

Due to the dramatic cuts of garments, “cut-out” fashion is usually labeled as the “controversial trend.” Not only because it’s a daring style, but also because it’s said to be a style that only certain body types “can” wear. Within one piece of clothing, there can be multiple cuts and some cuts can be very provocative. This also hinders the flexible applicability of the clothing piece for the wearer. If it’s not the right size and fit, the piece will not reveal the part that it’s supposed to reveal which can be irritating.

It’s interesting to note how though there are many parts being cut-out, the piece still covers well. It’s a definitely a popular style amongst celebrities.
