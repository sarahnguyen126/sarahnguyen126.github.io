As we go into 2023, “baggy fashion” is still one of the biggest trends in the fashion world. With this style, the wearer gets to drown themself in layers of garments. The style offers comfort yet still provides style.

Because this style allows the wearer to be as flexible with their silhouette as they desire, the style doesn’t discriminate against anyone. It’s a style that every body type looks good in, providing a more inclusive space in fashion. A trendy style that isn't the cost of your self-esteem. Some of the most popular baggy garments include: wide jeans, flared bottoms, oversized shirts, oversized hoodies, and oversized sweaters.
